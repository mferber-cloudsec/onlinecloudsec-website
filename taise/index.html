What is TAISE?

The definitive framework for AI security professionals

TAISE (Trustworthy AI Security & Ethics) is a certification program developed by the Cloud Security Alliance and Northeastern University. It provides a comprehensive framework for understanding and implementing responsible AI practices in organizations.

The 10 TAISE Domains

1. AI Governance

Establishing organizational structures and policies for AI oversight

2. Risk Management

Identifying and mitigating AI-specific risks

3. Data Security

Protecting data throughout the AI lifecycle

4. Model Security

Securing AI models from attacks and misuse

5. Infrastructure Security

Protecting AI computing environments

6. Application Security

Securing AI-powered applications

7. Ethics & Fairness

Ensuring AI systems are fair and unbiased

8. Privacy

Protecting individual privacy in AI systems

9. Resilience

Building robust and reliable AI systems

10. Compliance

Meeting regulatory requirements for AI
