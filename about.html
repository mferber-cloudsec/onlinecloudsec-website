About Me

Cloud security pioneer and advocate for responsible technology adoption

Cloud Security Evangelist & AI Safety Expert

Moshe Ferber is a recognized industry expert, popular public speaker and thought leader in the Israeli innovation community, with over 25 years' experience at various positions. Currently focusing on cloud security as entrepreneur and lecturer, promoting responsible cloud adoption by developing the next generation of cloud security best practices for the industry.

As Chairman of the Cloud Security Alliance Israel Chapter, I work to advance cloud security standards and best practices in the region and globally.

Professional Background

  • Chairman - Cloud Security Alliance (CSA) Israel Chapter
  • Certification Contributor - TAISE, CCSP, CCSK, CCAK
  • Certified Trainer - CSA and ISC2
  • Board Member - Dona-Tech and Machshava Tova (nonprofit)
  • Angel Investor - Cyber and enterprise applications
