Training Classes

Certification preparation and custom training programs

TAISE Certification Training

Duration: 2-3 days | Format: In-person or Virtual

Comprehensive preparation for the TAISE (Trustworthy AI Security & Ethics) certification. Covers all 10 domains with hands-on exercises and exam preparation. Led by a TAISE certification contributor.

CCSP Certification Prep

Duration: 5 days | Format: In-person or Virtual

Prepare for the (ISC)² Certified Cloud Security Professional exam. Comprehensive coverage of all domains with practice questions and exam strategies. As a CCSP founding committee member, I provide unique insights into the certification.

CCSK Training

Duration: 2 days | Format: In-person or Virtual

Certificate of Cloud Security Knowledge (CCSK) preparation. Foundation-level cloud security certification from the Cloud Security Alliance. Perfect for those starting their cloud security journey.

Custom Corporate Training

Duration: Flexible | Format: In-person or Virtual

Tailored training programs for your organization's specific needs. Topics include cloud security architecture, threat modeling, compliance, and security awareness. Content customized to your industry and technical level.
